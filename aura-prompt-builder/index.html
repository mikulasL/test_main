Hopsa

Discipline
Packaging
Year
2025
Role
Packaging & wordmark

Packaging and a bouncy wordmark for a kids’ fruit-snack brand sold across Czech supermarkets.

The brief

Stand out on a crowded shelf, appeal to kids, and still reassure the parents doing the buying. The range had to scale to new flavours without redrawing the whole system each time.

The outcome

A playful but legible wordmark and a colour-coded range that scales cleanly to new flavours. Bright enough for children, clean enough that parents trust the contents.

A little about me.

I’m Mikuláš Linhart, a 23 year old graphic designer based in Brno, Czechia. I help founders, studios and small teams shape brands that look sharp and actually hold together, from the first logo to a full launch.

I started out drawing on things I probably shouldn’t have, studied graphic design, and now get to do it for a living. My favourite work sits right where clear systems meet a bit of personality.
